# refcovers — your style examples

Put 2–3 screenshots of YouTube covers whose STYLE you want to match here (e.g. the
"solid dark bg + bright highlight box on a key word + face on the right" look).

The skill feeds two of these (plus your face photos and your logo) into gpt-image so the
model copies the style instead of inventing one. Use your own examples — none are bundled.
